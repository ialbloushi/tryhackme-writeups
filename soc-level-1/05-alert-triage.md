## Alert Prioritisation

This task focused on determining which alerts should be handled first.

### Approach

* Prioritise based on severity (Critical → High → Medium → Low)
* Focus on unassigned alerts
* Consider older alerts as potential ongoing attacks

### Results

* Prioritise medium over low: Yes
* Investigate newest alerts first: No

### First Priority Alert

* Potential Data Exfiltration
